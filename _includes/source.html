About Us

BDE Preworkout came about through my own experience navigating the supplement world. I enjoyed trying new mixes, it made me excited to hit the gym and see what I could do with it. But sometimes it'd made me agitated. Other times made for sleepless nights. Some had so much caffeine that it felt like a panic attack. Looking at the nutrition label for guidance often was a source of frustration, specific quantities of caffeine or beta-alanine hidden behind "proprietary blends", providing only the sum total of all the supplements. In an effort to dial back the adverse side effects I developed this tool to create the best mix for anybody, while also being open, honest, and clear about what's inside.
